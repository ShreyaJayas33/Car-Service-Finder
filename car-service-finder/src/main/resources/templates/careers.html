Join Our Team at Pacman Eatery

We are always looking for talented individuals to join our team. Check out our open positions below and click on the job title to see the full description, responsibilities, and requirements.

Open Positions:

  • Chefs
  • Waiters
  • Managers
  • Fryers
  • Cleaners

Chefs

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare a variety of Indian vegetarian dishes with focus on quality and flavor.
  • Maintain a clean and organized kitchen workspace.
  • Work closely with the kitchen staff to ensure timely and quality food service.
  • Follow food safety standards and procedures.

Requirements:

  • Previous experience as a chef, preferably in Indian cuisine.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Strong knowledge of food safety and sanitation standards.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.

Waiters

Responsibilities:

  • Take orders and serve food and beverages to customers efficiently.
  • Ensure customer satisfaction by providing friendly and professional service.
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of the dining area.
  • Handle customer inquiries and special requests.

Requirements:

  • Previous experience in a restaurant or hospitality setting preferred.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work well under pressure in a fast-paced environment.

Managers

Responsibilities:

  • Oversee daily operations of the restaurant.
  • Manage staff schedules and ensure optimal performance.
  • Monitor inventory and coordinate with suppliers.
  • Ensure customer satisfaction and handle any complaints.
  • Implement and maintain health and safety regulations.

Requirements:

  • Previous experience in restaurant management.
  • Strong leadership and organizational skills.
  • Excellent communication and problem-solving abilities.

Fryers

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and fry food items according to recipes and restaurant standards.
  • Maintain cleanliness and safety of the fry station.
  • Monitor food quality and ensure dishes are served hot and fresh.
  • Work in a fast-paced environment and collaborate with kitchen staff.

Requirements:

  • Experience in frying or cooking in a restaurant setting preferred.
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to follow recipes accurately.
  • Ability to work in a team and take direction from kitchen leads.

Cleaners

Responsibilities:

  • Maintain cleanliness in all areas of the restaurant, including the kitchen, dining area, and restrooms.
  • Follow cleaning schedules and ensure sanitation standards are met.
  • Dispose of waste and ensure the restaurant is presentable at all times.

Requirements:

  • Previous experience in cleaning or janitorial roles preferred.
  • Ability to work efficiently and follow instructions.
  • Strong attention to detail and commitment to maintaining cleanliness.
